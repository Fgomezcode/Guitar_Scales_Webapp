KEY AND CHORD PROGRESSION GENERATOR

Enter a ROOT NOTE and preferred TUNING. You can enter NATURAL or SHARP notes ( D# not Eb).

TUNING can be input as one string 'C#F#BEG#C#' or with spaces 'e a d g b e'.

Enter tuning from LOW to HIGH.
The Number of strings is determined by the instruments tuning.

This application will generate 41 KEYS and 19 PROGRESSIONS along with a FRETBOARD displays the notes of the current key.
